What we do

We Offer the BEST Services

Audio Solution

Having the right sound is an indispensable prerequisite for the success of any event. No matter the size or location of your event, indoor or outdoor our top-of-the line audio equipment and specialist team of sound engineers guarantees the audience will receive your message crystal clear. With one of the largest audio inventories in East Africa we offer a wide array of state of the art audio solutions to fit your event needs.

Lighting Solution

We have an impressive array of inventory to handle the illumination needs for your event and our lighting designers help turn your vision into a reality, creating custom lighting solutions that heighten the event experience for your audience. Using the latest technology, our experienced team will find the perfect solution for your event and stage lighting needs by harnessing the power of artistic illumination to add drama, impact and emotion to big and small productions alike.

Visual Display Solution

Our state-of-the-art Daylight LED screens, cinema-grade projectors, high-definition displays and innovative screen configurations can transform any space into an immersive environment. The vivid resolution, high performance and ultra-high brightness of the Daylight LED screens enable everyone in your audience to catch the action wherever they may be seated and regardless of weather conditions. Our video Display Solutions engineers are experts at bringing your ideas to life in a visually dynamic way through a variety of display solutions. Memorable results are obtained by incorporating video playback as the core content delivery system, supported by stunning and creative stage design.

Event Set/Design and Scenic

Our set design and scenic team is able to take your idea and transform it into spectacular reality to make your event professional and truly memorable. We will listen and help you create that reality utilizing two and three dimensional renderings to enhance the creative process. We welcome the opportunity to partner with our clients early in the process in order to fully realize their vision.
